Olga Tertitsa

Product Designer at Blispay

Olga is a product designer based in Baltimore, MD. She’s part of the team at Blispay, a fintech startup enabling merchants to grow their business’ through offering customers financing. There, she creates end-to-end design solutions for Blispay’s digital products, as well as focuses on the launch experience for merchants.

Previously, she worked as a designer on the marketing team at OrderUp, a restaurant food delivery startup that was acquired by Groupon.

When she’s away from the office, she can be found in her kitchen cooking dinner, at a local bar for her weekly trivia night, hanging out with the cats at the Maryland SCPA, or out in the woods somewhere camping or hiking with her boyfriend.
